ABOUT ME

Michael Takeuchi

Location: Phoenix, AZ

mikemtaki@gmail.com

Growing up I always dreamt about working with money. Loved the idea of knowing how people and companies were gaining and spending money. I studied Accounting and Economics in school to finally get closer to that dream becoming a reality. And then I got my first internship, and everything was working kind of became more dull by the day. I started a new career change in Sales to see if it was the right fit for me after seeing that finance was not my calling as I thought. My experience in Sales changed my outlook on the working environment and how to properly conduct myself in a professional setting. I had to get accustomed to constant changes in deadlines and plans while working with clients. Also needed to prioritize tasks that management wanted accomplished. And most importantly, I vastly improved on how to communicate with my team, co-workers, and clients.

However I still enjoyed learning, and eventually I got interested in programming. I started asking around to friends working as developers about their jobs, and also what programming was like. Everything started to roll when I began my first coding lesson online and learning about HTML and CSS. Seeing a website come to life just from some lines, blew my mind. I was hooked. I quickly moved onto Javascript and gained a strong understanding of problem solving and the ability to interact with a website.

At this point I knew I found what I wanted to do for my career. Within a year of beginning my transition to software engineering, I enrolled into Flatiron School, to develop the skills and knowledge as a developer. Through this course, I have gained and expanded my skills in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, React, and Git/GitHub. I have greatly enjoyed my time throughout the course, and look forward to continuing to further my skills as well as learn new programming languages.
